SECURITIES AND DATA RETENTION

Jobs Pal Ltd. is committed to protecting the privacy of all users of our service. We will endeavour to ensure that all information you provide us remains private and that it is only used for the purposes set out in this policy.

SECURITY

Jobs Pal Ltd. takes every precaution to protect your personal data. Access to users' personal information, is restricted to employees of Jobs Pal Ltd. All personal data is stored within our secure computer network.

CANDIDATE DATABASE

When you register for a vacancy with Jobs Pal Ltd, your personal data will be held on our central recruitment database. Under the Employment Agencies Act, Jobs Pal Ltd. is obliged to hold your data for 12 months after it was last used. If at any time you wish your details to be removed from our database, please email us and we will archive your details from our database.

We are obliged to follow the principles set out in the Act when we process your personal data. For the purpose of the Data Protection Act, Jobs Pal Ltd. will act as a “data controller.”
